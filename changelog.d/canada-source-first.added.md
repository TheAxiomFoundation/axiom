Added a source-first Canadian federal statute adapter (`extract-canada-acts`) that downloads consolidated Acts from laws-lois.justice.gc.ca, parses LIMS XML, and emits canonical citation paths (`canada/statute/{consolidated_number}/{section}/{label}`) with deterministic IDs. Replaces a legacy ingest path that left `corpus.provisions.citation_path` null on every Canadian row. Switches the underlying download to `requests` to work around an `httpx` SSL hang on large act XMLs.
